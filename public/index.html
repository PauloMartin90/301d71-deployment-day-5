About Me

Hiya! My name is Paulo P. Martin I’m a veteran environmentalist, bitcoiner, and machine learning enthusiast. I’m a software developer from Fresno, Ca originally coming from Naga City, Philippines. My professional background spans from mesh-networking start-ups and bitcoin trading in the Philippines, working as a crew manager build stages at festivals, working press at Humboldt radio stations, and even working and maintain an armory in Camp Casey, South Korea near the border. I was originally going to school for environmental engineering and wanted to use my equations I learned to impact the world. So I decided during the pandemic to put down school for now and dive headfirst into the world of Machine Learning. I hope to use my newfound skills in TensorFlow to create an environmentally clean future one line of code at a time.
